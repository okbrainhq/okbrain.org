---
title: Users & Sharing
---

OKBrain is a multi-user system where multiple people can use it at the same time. However, these users must be trusted, since they share access to:

* Events / Calendar
* Shell execution environment

You can also share apps you've created, making them available to all users.

## Conversations/Docs Sharing

Beyond the multi-user setup, you can share conversations and documents publicly. After sharing, you'll get a short URL that anyone can access.

> When sharing a conversation, we remove thoughts and tool call logs. However, the conversation itself may still contain sensitive information. Be mindful of what you share.
