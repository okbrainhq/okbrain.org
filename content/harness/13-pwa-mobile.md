---
title: PWA & Mobile App
---

OKBrain Harness is a web app configured as a Progressive Web Application (PWA). You can add it to your home screen, and when you open it, it behaves as a full-screen app without the URL bar.

Since it is a simple Next.js web app, load times are very fast. You can create an iPhone shortcut that loads the app and assign it to the iPhone Action Button, giving you a dedicated hardware launcher for OKBrain Harness.
