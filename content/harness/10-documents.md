---
title: Documents Support
---

OKBrain has a simple, Notion-like document editor where you can keep notes and documentation. By design, AI models cannot write or edit documents — it's a document system for you.

We believe writing is a core part of the learning process for humans. If we outsource that, it will eventually affect us negatively. That's why AI models can't edit documents.

However, you can ask questions based on a document. This helps you research based on what you've written, and then update the document as needed.

Documents support snapshots for versioning and can be shared via a public URL.
